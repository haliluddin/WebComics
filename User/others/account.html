Deleting your account on WEBCOMIC is permanent and cannot be undone. If you delete your account, the following information will immediately and permanently be deleted and irrecoverable:

  1. Information you provide when you created a WEBCOMIC account, including but not limited to email address, username, and authentication credentials used to sign up or log in

  2. All of your remaining Coin Balance

  3. All of your purchased episodes

  4. All of your reading histories, comments, and activities

  5. All of your series & episodes you created as a creator


If you generated a revenue as a creator, you cannot cash out remaining profit after deleting your account.
